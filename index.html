Why Digital Security is the Future

In an era where everything is connected, staying safe online is more critical than ever. Hackers, scams, and data breaches threaten our digital lives. Be prepared and stay one step ahead.

Recent studies show 70% of teens have faced cyber threats (CyberSafe 2023). Protect yourself by using strong passwords, updates, and privacy controls.

Ask the CyberWiz!

How can I tell if a message is a scam?

Be cautious of urgent requests, check sender details, and avoid clicking suspicious links. Always verify with trusted sources or ask an adult.

How do I make my social media private?

Go to your privacy settings, set your profile to private, and review who can see your posts regularly.

How can I keep my passwords safe?

Create strong, unique passwords, consider using a password manager, and enable two-factor authentication.
