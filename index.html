In today’s world online safety and security is more important than anything else. The purpose of this website is to alert everyone about common dangers online and provide helpful advice onto how to protect your privacy and stay safe online.

Online safety and security involve applying strategies to safeguard your personal information, avoid threats and maintaining privacy. As technology becomes more used in our lives understanding how to stay safe is key to prevent cyberbullying and any other online dangers

Why Online Safety and Security Matter

In today’s world protecting yourself online is vital with increasing cyberthreats such as hacking and identity theft, young people must understand the importance of protecting their personal information. Being always careful can help prevent any dangers online and if not taken care of properly then there can be some big consequences.

Research indicates that most young people are vulnerable on the internet. Indeed, the Boston Consulting Group and Global Cybersecurity Forum in their 2022 report discovered that approximately 72% of children and teenagers who use the internet have been targeted by at least one form of cyber threat, 78% of 16- to 18-year-olds being among them. It has also been found through research that most of the young online users habitually post personal information like their name, birthday, or school on the internet, and thus they are at risk of being hurt if they are not extremely sensitive to the repercussions. These discoveries indicate the critical need for proper online security measures to safeguard one's data and be secure on the web.

By knowing how to use secure passwords, identify phishing scams, and activate privacy settings, young online citizens can minimize their vulnerability on the internet and have a safer online life.

Ask the Cyber Guardian!

Hey Cyber Guardian, I keep getting friend requests from strangers—should I accept or ignore?

Great Question. online friend requests are like making friends in real life. The person must be trustworthy and someone you know such as a person from school. you wont just go make friends with a stranger from the street that you just met. if the person sending the friend request is a stranger or a bit sketchy then it is best to block that person. Only accept people you may know or people who are trustworthy.

I received an email asking for my personal details. How can I tell if it's a scam or legit?

Nice Catch! Scams usually want to make you panic and do something wrong so they can hack you or steal your personal details by saying ACT NOW! or have a countdown timer to scare you. The best way to act here is to check the senders email, usually scammers use fake addresses that look very real. just make sure not to click on the links and try block them if they are suspicious.

What’s one easy way to protect my online accounts from hackers?

Easy, use strong and diffrent passwords for diffrent apps or websites, never use the same one. Use 2 factor authentification which makes it 10 times harder for hackers to hack you. You can also use password managers so you never forget your passwords.
