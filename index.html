Why Online Security Matters

In today's digital age, safeguarding your online information is more important than ever. Cyber threats like hacking, phishing, and identity theft can have serious consequences if you're not careful.

Statistics show that over 60% of teens have experienced some form of online privacy breach (CyberSafe Youth Report, 2022). Protecting your passwords and personal info is crucial.

Ask the Online Safety Wiz!

Dear Wiz, I often get suspicious messages asking for my personal info. How do I know if they’re scams?

Great question! Never share personal info like passwords or bank details. Look out for strange language or urgent requests. When in doubt, ask a trusted adult or check official websites.

Dear Wiz, I want to make my social media accounts more private. How can I do that?

Easy! Go into your account settings, find privacy options, and set your profiles to private. Limit who can see your posts and personal info to trusted friends.

Dear Wiz, I’m worried about my passwords being hacked. Any tips?

Use strong, unique passwords for each account, and consider a password manager. Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible for extra protection.
