In our hyper-connected world, managing screen time and digital habits is crucial for maintaining mental and physical health. This site aims to help you find a healthy balance between online activities and offline well-being.

Why Managing Screen Time Matters

Excessive screen time can negatively affect your sleep, mood, and productivity. According to the World Health Organization, children and teens should limit recreational screen time to ensure healthy development. Finding a balance helps improve focus, sleep quality, and mental health.

Research shows that teens who manage their screen time experience less anxiety and better sleep patterns (Source: CDC, 2022). Setting boundaries and taking digital detox breaks are effective strategies.

Ask the Wellbeing Wiz!

Q1: Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I spend 5 hours on TikTok every night and feel exhausted. How can I cut back without missing out?

Hi! Try setting a timer or using app limits to control your usage. Find alternative activities like reading or exercising. Remember, moderation is key—enjoy your apps without overdoing it!

Q2: Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I feel anxious when I don't check my phone. How can I reduce this dependency?

Hello! Start by turning off non-essential notifications and setting specific times to check your phone. Practice mindfulness and engage in offline hobbies to build healthier habits.

Q3: Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I find it hard to sleep because I’m glued to my devices at night. Any tips?

Hi! Establish a tech-free hour before bed. Use blue light filters and create a relaxing bedtime routine to improve your sleep quality.
